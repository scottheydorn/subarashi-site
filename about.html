Our Story

Subarashi was founded with a vision to help businesses successfully navigate the complex world of distributed systems and digital optimization. Subarashī is derived from 'subarashii' meaning "brilliant" or "wonderful" in Japanese, reflects our commitment to excellence in everything we do.

Based in Virginia, USA, Subarashi specializes in helping companies optimize their application lifecycle, market and manage solutions being introduced to the US market with a pragmatic focus on improving our client's customers experience and satisfaction.

Our Approach

At Subarashi, we believe in a systematic, data-driven approach to consulting. We are familiar with and leverage a variety of development and delivery frameworks that best fit the desired outcome.

Our team brings together expertise in market research, go-to-market/strategic planning, product development and operations, providing comprehensive support at every stage of your growth journey.

People

Scott Heydorn

Scott is a seasoned technology leader with over 13 years of experience driving digital transformation and managing complex technical programs. As the founder of Subarashi LLC, Scott brings extensive expertise in strategic technology consulting, product management, portfolio/program delivery, API development, cloud infrastructure, and Agile methodology to help clients better understand and optimize their development and delivery processes. Prior to establishing Subarashi LLC, Scott served as a Distinguished Consultant at Modus Create, where he provided executive-level engineering leadership and developed strategic technology roadmaps for enterprise clients. His career includes senior leadership roles at Ellucian, where he directed global engineering teams serving over 2,500 higher education institutions, and at The College Board, where he oversaw major digitization initiatives for AP and SAT programs. Throughout his career, Scott has demonstrated excellence in aligning technology solutions with business objectives, with start ups as well as large, established technology firms. His technical expertise spans cloud infrastructure implementations, DevOps optimization, API development, application security, system architecture. Scott proudly served in the United States Navy, where he honed his leadership skills and disciplined approach to problem-solving. His military experience provided a strong foundation in digital communications, logistics, team coordination, and operating under pressure in diverse physical and cultural environments – skills he continues to apply when helping clients navigate complex business challenges and technology implementations. Scott specializes in helping businesses navigate complex technology transitions, particularly in heterogenous environments that are dependent upon legacy technology but wish to leverage modern approaches. His consultative approach focuses on understanding each client's unique challenges and developing tailored solutions that drive efficiency and growth. Based in Chantilly, Virginia, Scott likes to spend his spare time with his family, working on vintage electronics and a 20 year old Jeep TJ.
